The Pico Labs Affiliate Network (PLAN) is operated by Bruce Conrad, a supporter of Pico Labs.

There is no service level agreement (SLA). The contributors, committers and maintainers of this open source code repository (linked in the previous paragraph) intend to devote their best effort to making PLAN as reliable/safe/secure/responsive as humanly possible.

We recognize that you have a choice of personal agent hosting sites and appreciate your affiliation.

For more information about Pico Labs, visit its home page at picolabs.io or send an email message.
